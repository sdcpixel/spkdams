Project Overview

Shahpurkandi Dam Project is a major hydropower and irrigation project constructed on the River Ravi in Pathankot District, Punjab. The project aims to utilize the available water resources for irrigation, power generation, and regional development.

Objectives of the Project

  • Optimal utilization of Ravi river waters
  • Generation of hydroelectric power
  • Irrigation benefits to Punjab agriculture
  • Employment and regional development
